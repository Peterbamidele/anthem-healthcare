What do we expect from you?

We are lookign for highly motivated, dynamic candidates for the following top paying trainee jobs.

Education

There are routes into web development for both graduates and school leavers. Graduates with a degree in computer science, software development or similar are preferred for many junior web developer roles but it possible to find a graduate web developer job whatever your degree background, as long as you have some practical knowledge of web development processes and languages.

Essential Skills

Good front-end development knowledge, Creation of prototypes from ideas and mock-ups from wire frames, HTML5 / CSS3 / Javascript ES6, Deep knowledge in Test-Driven Development, Experience of full software lifecycle from interpretation of requirements to testing and deployment to production, Solid understanding of object orientated design skills through application of enterprise patterns and best practices.

Communication Skills

Strong analytical, problem solving, written and verbal communication skills, Ability to liaise with client management and technical staff and be face-of-the-company in respect of technical and developmental matters, Ability to be part of multiple, simultaneous work streams.

Duties

Work closely with designers, product managers and back-end engineers to implement versatile front-end solutions for complicated UX requirements, Embrace emerging standards while promoting best practices, Ensuring high application quality and performance, Launch, iterate and make a difference. Provide front-end coding expertise and be ready to make our products better for our users, Cooperating with the back-end developer.

Location

We are based in Watford. The candidate is expected to be in our office all weekdays between 9:00am and 5pm. there may be occational on call support required.
